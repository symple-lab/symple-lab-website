# SYMPLE Lab Website Redesign Notes

## Design Reference: CAST Lab (castlab.kaist.ac.kr)

### Key Design Elements Observed:
1. **Navigation**: Top horizontal navigation bar with text links (About Us, Research, Publications, People, Events, Chip Gallery)
2. **Color Scheme**: Bright green accent color (#00FF00 or similar) with clean white/light backgrounds
3. **Typography**: Modern sans-serif fonts, clean and readable
4. **Layout**: Minimal use of boxes, instead uses horizontal lines and dashed borders
5. **Visual Style**: Clean, academic, professional - no heavy shadows or complex geometric shapes
6. **Publication Display**: Simple list format with category tags (Circuit, Architecture, etc.)
7. **News Section**: Horizontal dashed border boxes for news items
8. **Overall Aesthetic**: Minimalist, tech-forward, academic

## SYMPLE Lab Redesign Plan

### Color Palette:
- **Primary**: Bright Blue (#0066FF or #0047AB - but lighter variants)
- **Secondary**: Light Blue (#E8F4FF or #F0F8FF)
- **Accent**: Bright Green (#00FF00 or #00DD00) - for highlights and tags
- **Text**: Dark Gray (#333333) or Black (#000000)
- **Background**: White (#FFFFFF) or very light gray (#F9F9F9)
- **Borders**: Light gray (#DDDDDD) or dashed lines

### Navigation Structure:
- **Top horizontal navigation bar** (not left sidebar)
- Text-based menu items: About Us / Research / Publications / People / Chip Gallery
- Clean, minimal styling

### Page Structure:

#### Main Pages:
1. **Home** - Hero section with lab description
2. **About Us** - Lab mission, vision, research overview
3. **Research** - Research areas and focus
4. **Publications** - Hub page with links to sub-pages
5. **People** - Hub page with links to sub-pages
6. **Chip Gallery** - Showcase of chip designs

#### Publications Sub-pages:
- Paper
- Patent
- Award
- News

#### People Sub-pages:
- Professor
- Student
- Gallery

### Design Principles:
- Minimize box usage - use horizontal lines instead
- Rounded corners where necessary (not sharp angles)
- Clean typography hierarchy
- Ample whitespace
- Accent colors for highlights and tags
- Simple, professional aesthetic
- Academic credibility through clean design
